What We Do

At Rig Rater, we focus on:

  • In-Depth Analysis: We meticulously research and aggregate data from reputable tech reviewers, benchmark databases, and manufacturer specifications to provide comprehensive insights beyond simple spec sheets.
  • Clear Comparisons: We synthesize information to offer clear comparisons, highlighting the pros and cons of components based on consensus and available data.
  • Guides & How-Tos: From choosing the best budget GPU to understanding motherboard chipsets, we create guides to demystify PC building using the best available information.
  • Value Focus: While we cover high-end gear, we're always looking for the best value, helping you maximize performance without emptying your wallet, based on market trends and performance data.
  • Transparency: We clearly disclose how we operate, including our use of affiliate links (see our Affiliate Disclosure). Our recommendations are driven by diligent research and analysis, not commissions.

The Rig Rater Approach

Building a great PC requires trustworthy information. Our approach centers on:

  • Extensive Research: We scour the web, consult leading tech publications (like GamersNexus, Tom's Hardware, RTINGS.com, etc.), analyze benchmark results, and study manufacturer data.
  • Data Synthesis: We compile and compare information from multiple sources to identify performance trends, feature sets, and potential issues.
  • Clear Presentation: We strive to present our findings in an easy-to-understand format, cutting through jargon and focusing on what matters most to builders and gamers.
  • Honest Evaluation: While we don't perform hands-on testing for every item ourselves in this current model, our conclusions are based on a thorough analysis of the available expert data and community feedback.

Our goal is to be a reliable resource by doing the heavy lifting of research and analysis, whether you're building your first PC or upgrading a seasoned battle station.
